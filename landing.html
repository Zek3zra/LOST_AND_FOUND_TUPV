About Retrieve TUPV

Retrieve TUPV is the official, centralized lost-and-found repository designed exclusively for the Technological University of the Philippines Visayas campus.

Our mission is to foster a community of honesty, trust, and security by providing a reliable digital platform where students, faculty, and staff can easily report missing valuables or turnover items they have found.

Built with efficiency and privacy in mind, this system directly connects the campus community with the Administration Office to ensure items are returned to their rightful owners safely and swiftly.

How It Works

1. Submit a Report

Use your dashboard to report an item you have lost or found. Provide accurate details. If you found an item, physically turn it over to the Administration Office.

2. Administrator Review

The TUPV Admin will review your submission. Once verified, the item will be posted to the public feed for the campus community to see.

3. Verification & Claiming

If you see your lost item on the feed, visit the Admin Office and provide your "Secret Identifiers" (proof of ownership). Once verified, the item will be marked as matched and safely returned to you!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I claim an item?

Go to the TUPV Administration Office with your Student/Faculty ID. You will be asked to describe specific details about the item that only the true owner would know.

Why is my report still "Pending"?

Found items remain pending until they are physically turned over to the Admin. Lost items require Admin review before they are published to prevent spam.

Can I delete a report?

Users cannot delete active reports to maintain system integrity. If you need a report removed, please use the Support Chat to contact the Administrator.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how Retrieve TUPV handles your data.

Data Collection

We collect basic directory information (Name, Course, Contact Number) solely to facilitate the returning of lost items. We do not track your location or browsing habits outside of the application.

Data Usage & Visibility

Your contact details are strictly hidden from the public feed. Only the System Administrator can view your full profile to verify ownership and send system notifications.

Data Protection

All user data is encrypted and securely stored using modern authentication standards. We will never sell or share your personal information with third-party entities.

Terms of Service

By accessing and using Retrieve TUPV, you agree to comply with the following campus guidelines:

  • Honesty Policy: Attempting to claim an item that does not belong to you is a severe violation. False claims will be reported directly to the Disciplinary Board.
  • Accuracy: All reports submitted must be truthful and accurate to the best of your knowledge. Spamming or posting fake items will result in immediate account suspension.
  • Liability: While Retrieve TUPV facilitates the recovery process, the platform and the University are not liable for items that are permanently lost, stolen, or damaged prior to turnover.
